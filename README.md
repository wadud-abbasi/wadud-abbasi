# 🎈 Abdul Wadud Abbasi

## Machine Learning Specialist and Data Analyst

Hi there, I am a research associate currently exploring the medicinal applications of machine learning/artificial intelligence using physics-informed neural networks (PINNs). I like to incorporate applied mathematics into my research pursuits, including but not limited to: Non-Newtonian Mechanics and Electrostatics, Convolutions, Fourier/Laplace Transform, and other discrete algorithms. Most of my scripts are not published for publicity, but I wish to use GitHub as a space to contribute to the democratization of free information and learning.

I am particularly interested in studying electrical phenomena for RNNs and pharmacogenomic specificity. I believe that everyone deserves medications tailored to their specific symptoms without third-party informants, which is why I am vetted to understand the micro/macro aspects of medicine.
